Wound Bed Preparation (WBP) 2021

Wound Bed Preparation (WBP) 2021

🏠 Home 📋 Patient Dashboard 📊 Wound Tracking 📈 Healing Analysis 📝 New Assessment

Treat the Cause

Address the underlying conditions affecting wound healing, such as pressure relief, blood flow, and comorbidities.

Patient-Centered Concerns

Consider pain management, lifestyle adjustments, and the patient’s overall health condition.

Wound Type

  • Healable: Wounds expected to heal with proper care.
  • Maintenance: Wounds requiring long-term management due to underlying conditions.
  • Non-Healable: Wounds that will not heal due to irreversible causes.

Good Wound Care

Debridement

Removal of dead, damaged, or infected tissue to improve wound healing.

Infection & Inflammation Control

Use of antibiotics, antiseptics, and proper dressings to control bacterial growth and inflammation.

Moisture Balance

Ensuring the wound is neither too dry nor too moist for optimal healing.

Edges

Promoting healthy wound edges to encourage proper closure and reduce the risk of chronic wounds.

Evaluation

Regular reassessment to ensure treatment effectiveness and make necessary adjustments.
